Chalkboard Privacy Policy

Effective date: 9 July 2026

Information the App Uses

Chalkboard may access your location to display nearby climbing gyms. The app also stores profile information, favourites, visited gyms, wishlist items and settings locally on your device.

Data Storage

The developer does not collect or store your personal data on external servers. All personal information is stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Chalkboard uses Google Maps Platform services, including Google Maps, Google Places and the Geocoding API to display maps and climbing gym information.
